Prove to me this works. I will not be reading or reviewing code. Present
evidence in natural language showing what changed, what was tested, what
passed, what failed, and what could still break. Format:

**Changes Made:**

- [What changed and why]

**Tests Run:**

- [Test name]: [PASS/FAIL]

**Evidence:**

- [Concrete output, exit codes, screenshots]

**Potential Risks Not Verified:**

- [Component]: [Why it might break]
- [Plan to address]
- [Integration]: [Assumption made]
- [Verification needed]
